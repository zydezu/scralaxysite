Released 2017-05-27

0.1.0 was the first release of Super Mario Scralaxy. It was released on 27th May 2017.

Features

  • Removed 2 player mode
  • Luigi has a double jump instead of a dive
  • Wario has a ground slam that gives more damage
  • Waluigi added
  • Character swapping reworked
  • Menu open/closing speed increased
