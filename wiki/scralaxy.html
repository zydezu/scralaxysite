Super mario Scralaxy
Wiki page

Super Mario Scralaxy is the first game by the Scralaxy Team. It follows Mario and his friends as they try to rescue Princess Peach and stop an unknown force from merging the Mushroom Kingdom and Subcon.

Development was started by Scratch user Pickleodeon (known as Untitled-1 on other sites) on 21st May 2017. Although he left Scralaxy Team later that year, the rest of the team continued to develop the game.


History

Very little can be found as to how development of the game started, nor how a team was assembled. From what we can tell, the first version of the game was released on 21st May 2017. The game was never planned to be a Super Mario Galaxy clone, but more of a culmination of previous 3D Mario games, according to Pickleodeon.

Pickleodeon is now longer working with the team, and while he maintained the studio, he himself released very few updates. After a little while, he gave up on the game and dumped it to the currently small, and uncertain Scralaxy Team. The team took it in full stride and continued to cultivate and improve Super Mario Scralaxy!
