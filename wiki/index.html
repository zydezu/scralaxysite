So... Who and what exactly is the Scralaxy Team?

The Scralaxy Team is a ever-growing group of users on Scratch (scratch.mit.edu) who collaborate on many games, mostly inspired by Nintendo.

If you would like to read a more detailed history of the team, as well as some more in depth information and trivia, click here!

As of the 3rd June 2022, the team is actively developing six games:

  • Super Mario Scralaxy (SMScralaxy)
  • Super Mario Scratchy World (SMSW)
  • Mario Kart 8 Screluxe (MK8S)
  • Mario Party S (MPScratch)
  • Super Scratch Bros. Scrultimate (SSBS)
  • Paper Mario: Tear In Reality (PMTIR)
